HELLO AND WELCOME,

My name is Ethan Cornwell and my current focus is a career change from the pharmaceutical industry towards something that I've always had an deep found interest in, Software Development.

A little backstory, I was born and raised in Oregon. My family moved often but finally settled in Newport on the West Coast.

From an early age, my sister and I worked for our parents at our family owned plant nursery business. It was in Newport where I met my wife Maya and where we made the decision to move to Colorado in 2012.

In 2013, my daughter Harper was born and in 2017, my son Oliver.

From Aug. 2013 - Oct. 2021, I worked in an Aseptic cleanroom environment, producing and managing a sterile injectable product used to treat prostate cancer. Although I loved the work and the ability to impact the lives of others, there were many contributing factors which lead to my leaving the field.

Now comes a new exciting chapter in my life where I will be pursuing the knowledge and skills required for software development or something in the related field.
